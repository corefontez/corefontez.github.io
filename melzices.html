Redesigning

Ecommerce Website

Creating a more delightful experience with local ecommerce by redesigning the visual concept and the information architecture that provides everything the customer needs to purchase an electronic and accessories online.

Stakeholder: “ I need help! A customer just emailed me complaining that he couldn't finish the purchase on the website. What can you do for me?”

problem
Key Human Problem

We had evidence that the website violated some usability guidelines after user testings and a Heuristic Evaluation. On top of that, as a small, unknown business, the users don't trust the site to buy expensive equipment.

Key Business Problem

The user cannot successfully complete a task, in this case, the purchase. Hurting the sales results and decreasing the retention and conversion rate.

solution
Information Architecture Enhancements

Based on information architecture’s best practice, we could create an intuitive flow after research to understand the user’s mental model.

Increase Reliability

Since our reviews on Google and Facebook are 5 stars out of more than 200 comments for the outstanding service, we took the opportunity to create a section with recommendations of the past clients, eliminating the doubt of new users.

impact
Conversion Rate

After testing with 13 users, 83% of them said that the site would have everything they look for in an ecommerce and that they would probably finalize the purchase.

Increase Retention and Satisfaction rate

User feedback has proved improvement in qualitative results ratings for the overall website, including the delightful design and the percentage of users that said they would continue to use the website since they've found some usuful information.
