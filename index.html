About me

I am a Creative Product Designer

I've been designing digital experiences for over a year now across a variety of industries ( Fintech, e-commerce, eLearning ), collaborating with people who share the same passion and drive for making a real and positive impact in people's lives, solving complex problems, and learning together.

During this time, I've learned to be comfortable analyzing research insights, extracting insights from data, and developing innovative solutions. My field of interest lies in designing mobile and digital web products, thinking strategically, and analyzing the different possible outcomes.

After completing 2 UX/UI Designer courses in Berlin, I've decided to dive deeper into the UX design world by doing an Interaction Design for Digital Products post-graduate course starting in April - 2022 at the Innovation and Technology Centre in Brazil.

I'm a queer young designer who feels the necessity to develop an inclusive and accessible digital world for everybody by empathizing with the world around us. My goal is to create bridges to connect teams and make users be heard through design.
