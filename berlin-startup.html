Redesign website

Berlin Startup Map

Redesigning the Information Architecture ( IA ) and optimizing the entire section of the Public Service for SEO by creating accessible public information for Startup founders.

Stakeholder: “ The idea is to create “sexier” content to attract young entrepreneurs to bring their business to Berlin and facilitate finding investors.”

problem
Key Human Problem

Qualitative research and user testing showed users feeling overwhelmed when searching information. With a 100% bounce rate, the user spends less than a minute and gives up without completing a task.

Key Business Problem

With poor information architecture and no hierarchy, the site cannot give helpful information to the users, which is the main objective of the business.

solution
Information Architecture Enhancements

After research we could create an intuitive user flow based on information architecture best practices.

Visual Design Enhancements

We created a pleasing layout based on design patterns with hierarchy and divided pages by themes.

impact
Reduction in Bounce rate

Increasing the percentage of visitors who enter the site continuing to view other pages within the same site.

Increase in Ease-of-Use

User feedback has proved improvement in qualitative results ratings for the overall website.
