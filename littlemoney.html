Tracking app Design

Little Money

A tracking app to help parents teaching their children about money management with fun and lifelong learning by implementing responsibilities in daily life.

Challenge: to create a Native app from scratch to support parents in this challenging chore of teaching their kids how to get financially independent and enjoy the process.

problem
Key Human Problem

Market and user research has shown that parents of children aged between 6 and 12 find it difficult to teach their children how to manage money.

Key Business Problem

Teach children the value of money in a fun and intuitive way.

solution
Tasks and Chores

Creation of tasks and household chores with the possibility of earning money for children to learn the value of money.

Savings

creation of folders to save money simulating a savings with interest making them feel that they are earning money by saving.

impact
User acquisition

93% of parents interviewed said they needed an app to help them with these tasks.

Satisfaction

The final user testings showed an overall acceptance and satisfaction in the design and tasks
